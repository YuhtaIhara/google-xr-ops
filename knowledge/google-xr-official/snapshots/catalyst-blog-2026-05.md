## 抽出情報

### Program Overview

Google launched the Android XR Developer Catalyst Program to accelerate development of apps ready to launch within the next year across wired XR glasses and intelligent eyewear platforms.

### Key Resources Provided

- **Hardware**: Early access to development kits for XREAL's Project Aura and intelligent eyewear
- **Technical Support**: Specialized resources and dedicated support forums for Google Play preparation
- **Grants**: Non-recoupable funding available upon request to accelerate development

### Application Timeline

- **Deadline**: June 30, 2026, 11:59 PM PDT
- **Target Launch Window**: Within 6-12 months of application
- Result notification date: Not specified in announcement (landing page で 7-15)

### Eligibility Requirements

- Plans to publish apps for Android XR ecosystem within 6-12 months
- Can develop using **Kotlin/Jetpack XR SDK, Unity, Unreal Engine, or Godot**
- Welcome any unique use case

### "Innovative" Evaluation Criteria

The program expresses particular interest in: **media, gaming, productivity, and health sectors**, while remaining open to unique applications that "help users expand what's possible."
